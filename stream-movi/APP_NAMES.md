# Movie Streaming App - Name Suggestions

## Overview
A real-time synchronized movie streaming application that allows users to watch movies together.

## Suggested App Names

### Tier 1: Creative & Memorable
1. **StreamSync** - Emphasizes the synchronized streaming feature
2. **WatchParty** - Classic party watching concept
3. **CineTogether** - Movies together
4. **SyncFlix** - Netflix-style sync
5. **MovieMix** - Mixing movies with friends

### Tier 2: Fun & Playful
6. **PopcornParty** - Movie snack theme
7. **FlickSync** - Classic movie term + sync
8. **CineLink** - Linking cinema experiences
9. **StreamHub** - Central streaming hub
10. **WatchLoop** - Continuous watching together

### Tier 3: Professional & Clean
11. **StreamMates** - Streaming with mates/friends
12. **MovieSync** - Simple and direct
13. **TogetherWatch** - Descriptive
14. **GroupStream** - Group streaming
15. **SyncStream** - Sync + Stream

### Tier 4: Unique & Brandable
16. **StreamVibe** - Streaming vibe/energy
17. **CineSphere** - Cinema sphere/community
18. **WatchWave** - Wave of watchers
19. **FlickZone** - Movie zone
20. **StreamCircle** - Circular community

## Recommendation

**Top Pick: StreamSync**
- Easy to pronounce and remember
- Clearly communicates the core feature (synchronized streaming)
- Works well as a brand name
- Available domain potential

**Runner-up: WatchParty**
- Very intuitive
- Great for social media
- Instantly understood concept
